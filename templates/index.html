FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a social media manager?
No. Post-Pilot replaces the need for a dedicated social media manager for most small food businesses. The AI handles writing, scheduling, and publishing automatically.
Will my posts look generic?
No. You provide your business name, cuisine type, tone, and weekly specials. Every post is generated specifically for your business and never reused across accounts.
What happens if I don’t approve posts?
On auto-pilot mode, posts go live without approval. On review mode, you get a daily digest to approve or edit before anything publishes.
Can I connect multiple locations?
Yes — the Pro plan supports multi-location with separate content streams and platform connections per location.
Is my account data safe?
Yes. All platform connections use official OAuth APIs. We never store your social media passwords. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel, downgrade, or pause from billing settings at any time.
